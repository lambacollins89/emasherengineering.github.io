Core Competencies & Services

Electrical & Mechanical Works

Industrial electrical installations, maintenance, and fault finding. Mechanical system design, fabrication, installation, and repair.

Civil Works

Industrial building construction and renovation. Infrastructure development and maintenance. Concrete works, foundations, and structural repairs.

Mining Equipment & Tools

Supply of a wide range of mining equipment and specialized tools. Procurement and logistics for essential mining consumables.

Cleaning Services

Industrial-grade cleaning for workshops, plants, and commercial premises. Specialized cleaning for heavy machinery and equipment.

Hired Labour

Provision of skilled and semi-skilled labor for various industrial projects. Flexible staffing solutions to meet fluctuating project demands.

Fire Suppression Systems

Design, supply, and installation of state-of-the-art fire suppression systems for industrial applications. Routine inspection, testing, and maintenance.

Conveyor Belts & Rollers

Supply of high-quality conveyor belts and rollers for various material handling needs. Professional installation, splicing, and alignment.

Fluid Management Systems

Auto Lube on Machines: Design, supply, and installation of automatic lubrication systems. Wiggins Supply & Installation for efficient fluid transfer.

Component Repairs

Specialized repair and refurbishment of critical components for Loaders, Dump Trucks, Fuel Bowsers. Restoring components to OEM specifications.

Air Conditioning & Refrigeration

Installation, maintenance, and repair of industrial and commercial air conditioning systems. Refrigeration solutions for various applications.
